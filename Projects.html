3D Runner Game

"Boneyard Chase: Mummy's Quest for Humanity" is an immersive endless runner game in which players take on the role of an ancient Egyptian mummy determined to become human once again. The mummy is chased by hordes of skeletons trying to stop him and keep him in his eternal undead state.

In this game, you run through various challenging environments, ranging from dilapidated graveyards and ancient temples to deserted deserts. The player must dodge obstacles such as tombstones, traps, and hostile skeletons while collecting power-ups to accelerate their transformation into humanity.

The story unfolds as you progress, and players are drawn into the mystery of the mummy's curse and his quest for redemption. The game combines thrilling action, strategic decisions, and an immersive story to keep players engaged for hours as they try to help the mummy regain his humanity.

"Boneyard Chase: Mummy's Quest for Humanity" offers challenging gameplay, a captivating story, and stunning graphics to immerse players in the world of ancient Egypt and the supernatural as they race to help the mummy achieve his goal.

Made using Unity, C# and Blender.
