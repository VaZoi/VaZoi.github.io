Checker Game

Objective:

The objective of the game is to either capture all of your opponent's pieces or block them from making any legal moves.


Game Components:

The game board is represented as an 8x8 grid.

There are two players, one controlling white checkers and the other controlling black checkers.

Each player has 12 checkers at the beginning of the game.

Game Rules:

  1. Players take turns to move their checkers.
  2. White checkers move upward on the board, and black checkers move downward.
  3. A checker can move diagonally to an adjacent unoccupied square if no capturing move is available.
  4. A capturing move involves "jumping" over the opponent's checker in a diagonal direction and landing on an empty square immediately after the captured checker. After capturing, the player continues their turn and may make additional capturing moves if available.
  5. If a checker reaches the opposite end of the board, it becomes a "king." Kings have the ability to move and capture diagonally in any direction.
  6. The game continues until one player captures all of their opponent's checkers or when a player has no legal moves left.
